Project Detail

Credit Default Probability with Merton Model

This project applies structural finance models to estimate default probability and compare the credit risk profile of Ford and General Motors over a one-year period.

What It Does

Brief Overview

I used the Merton and Black-Scholes frameworks to estimate distance to default and probability of default, turning financial theory into a hands-on credit risk analysis project.
