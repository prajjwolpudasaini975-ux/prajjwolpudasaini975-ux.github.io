Project Detail

Student Performance Optimization Study

A Python-driven exploratory analysis of 6,600+ student records to identify what actually drives academic performance — surfacing thresholds, patterns, and actionable insights from education data.

Open Repository Back to Portfolio

What It Does

Brief Overview

I analyzed a dataset of 6,600+ student records to identify the key factors behind academic performance. The work combines statistical analysis, EDA, and visual storytelling to highlight where performance differences emerge and what thresholds matter most for outcomes.

Tools Used

Stack

  • Python
  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Statistical Analysis

Why It Matters

Business Value

The project shows how EDA can move beyond summary statistics to surface real patterns — the kind of analysis that helps institutions or educators prioritize interventions and allocate support where it will have the most impact.
